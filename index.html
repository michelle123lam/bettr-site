gamble your goals

Dreams can be hard to accomplish. We provide a platform for social betting, BETTR, where friends can establish wagers with one another to motivate self-improvement. Users can post their progress on their goal-oriented bets, and their friends can interact with their posts. With social support, we hope that people will help stay committed to their goals and cultivate discipline to Bettr their lives.
